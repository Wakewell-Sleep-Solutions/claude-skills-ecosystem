# Dental Practice Ad Creative & Paid Ads

Dental is the 2nd most expensive PPC vertical ($7.85 avg CPC, behind legal at $8.58). This skill generates high-converting ad copy and optimizes spend with real benchmark data.


**Step 0:** Read `.claude/context/practice-context.md` for practice overview, brand voice, patient pain points, and proof points before proceeding.

**Industry averages:** CTR 5.44% (lowest of all industries), conversion rate 9.08%, cost per lead $84, ROAS 5.5x. CPC rose 12.88% YoY in 2025.

### Quality Score Optimization (2026)
Google now incorporates Core Web Vitals into Quality Score. A Seattle clinic improved QS from 5→8, dropping CPC by 40% and gaining 30% more leads on same budget.

**Tactics:**
- Single-service ad groups (not broad "dental services")
- Semantic keyword variation ("permanent tooth replacement" + "dental implants")
- Landing page load under 3 seconds on mobile
- Aggressive negative keywords (DIY, free, cheap, jobs, school, salary)
- Ad extensions: location, call, sitelink, structured snippets

### Targeting
- Radius: 5-15 miles from practice
- Age: 35-65+ (implants/cosmetic), 25-45 (Invisalign), parents (pediatric)
- Life events: recently moved (need new dentist)
- Lookalike audiences from patient list (de-identify before upload per HIPAA)

## Landing Page Requirements

**Top dental landing pages convert 8-12%, elite campaigns 19-42%.**

| Must-Have | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| Procedure-specific page | Never send PPC to homepage |
| Message match to ad | "Same-Day Crown" in ad = same headline on page |
| Above-fold form (3 fields max) | Name, phone, email — minimal friction |
| Click-to-call phone (mobile) | 60%+ of dental searches are mobile |
| Social proof | Star ratings, testimonials, video reviews |
| Action CTA | "Book Your Free Consultation" not "Submit" |
| <3 sec load on 4G | Google factors CWV into Quality Score |

## Retargeting Strategies

- **Google Display:** Pixel → audience by pages visited → banner ads across millions of sites. Remarketing increases response up to 400%.
- **Facebook Custom Audiences:** Upload patient list for check-up reminders, new services. Pixel for website visitors by page.
- **Lookalike Audiences:** 1% = closest match, 10% = larger but less similar. One case study: eliminated wasted spend on wrong zip codes.
- **HIPAA caution:** Retargeting ads cannot contain medical vocabulary. Review privacy restrictions before launching.

## Compliance Guardrails

### HIPAA for Ads
- Written authorization required for patient photos in ads
- De-identify all before/after (crop to teeth only, strip metadata)
- Never reference specific patients by name

### Dental Board Regulations
- No guaranteed outcomes ("You WILL get perfect teeth" → ❌)
- No false urgency ("Only 2 spots left" must be true)
- Disclose offer conditions (free exam excludes X-rays → disclose)
- State-specific: GA requires disclaimers for atypical results

### Platform Policies
- **Google:** Dental = "moderately restricted" (Eligible Limited is normal, not a penalty). No LegitScript needed for standard dental. CWV now affects Quality Score.
- **Meta:** Before/after allowed with consent. Advantage+ may auto-modify visuals — monitor brand consistency.
- AI Overviews reduced paid CTR by 68% on informational queries — bid on transactional/booking intent keywords.

## A/B Testing Framework

| Variation | Angle | Example |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| A — Problem | Pain point | "Missing Teeth Affecting Your Confidence?" |

Run each 7+ days / 1,000+ impressions. Track CTR, CPC, and cost-per-conversion. A/B testing creative cuts CPA by 32%.
